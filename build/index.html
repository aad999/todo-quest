The Todo Quest

How about turning your boring to-do list into an epic adventure? Welcome to The Todo Quest, where every task is a challenge, and every challenge is a reward. Here’s how it works: you earn 20 coins for adding a task, and 100 coins for completing it. But be careful: you lose 50 coins for deleting a task. And if you go broke, you lose all your cards too. Yikes! You can spend your coins on awesome Marvel Superhero cards from the cards section. So, stay motivated and pursue your goals. Who wouldn’t want to be Captain America or Spider Man?
